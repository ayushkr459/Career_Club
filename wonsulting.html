Resources | Wonsulting

Wonsulting was created with one goal in mind: to turn underdogs into winners. Helping those who come from non-traditional backgrounds/non-target schools to get them into their dream careers. They've conducted 110+ workshops/events for universities/organizations both domestically in the United States and globally in other countries including Canada, India, Nigeria, UK, Australia, and Israel.

Wonsulting recently got nominated for Forbes 30 under 30. For more information check out the site at Wonsulting.com
